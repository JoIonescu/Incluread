## Reading for everyone <br /> <br />


IncluRead is an accessible reading platform designed to make digital content easier to read, understand, and retain for people with dyslexia, ADHD, visual stress, and other reading challenges.

The platform transforms traditional reading into a personalized experience through features such as:
- syllable breaking,
- bionic reading,
- assistive focus lines,
- sensory focus mode,
- word-by-word reading,
- chapter summaries,
- memory support cards,
- AI language assistant that helps explain unfamiliar words and sections.

Users can also simplify content at different levels and listen through customizable narrator voices and reading speeds.


IncluRead combines accessibility, AI, and cognitive support into a single reading workspace, helping readers focus on comprehension rather than decoding text.

📌 Disclaimer:
IncluRead is currently a work in progress. While the platform is fully functional in many areas, some features may still be under development or behave differently as improvements are introduced. New accessibility tools, refinements, and enhancements are being added regularly, so be sure to check back often to follow its evolution.
